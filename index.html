Augie adoption maturity

Where is your organisation today?

Every customer starts at Stage 0. The goal is Stage 4. Most stall at Stage 2. This framework gives your team and ours a shared language for the journey — and a shared commitment to completing it.

The problem Augment is solving internally: No account has yet reached 50% workflow penetration. Customers reach Stage 2 — stable implementation — and plateau. The gap between Stage 2 and Stage 3 is not a product problem. It is a change management and accountability problem. This framework closes it.

THE STALL PATTERN
FDE declares victory. CS inherits a cold account.
When CS doesn't know the account deeply at handoff, adoption stalls at Stage 2 and the expansion loop never starts. This is the root cause of zero accounts reaching 50% penetration.
WHAT CLOSES THE LOOP
CS feedback reaches Product. Leadership makes it a priority.
CS hears what customers need to advance. Product builds it. Leadership holds the pace. When that cycle runs fast, accounts move from Stage 2 to Stage 4.
THE CoS ROLE
Owns the crossover. Measures velocity through both loops.
The CoS sits above both loops — tracking handoff lag, accounts stuck at Stage 2, and where the loop is breaking. That is the weekly operating question that no one is currently asking.
What each role does — in order
AE
  • › Qualifies ICP fit and pain acuity before committing FDE resources
  • › Defines PoC success criteria in writing at contract signature
  • › Delivers implementation kickoff doc to CS before disengaging
›
CLIENT
  • › Names an internal champion with authority before go-live
  • › Dedicates ops team time to FDE diagnostic and SOP review
  • › Enforces zero-bypass discipline — escalate, never manually override
›
FDE
  • › Embeds on-site, maps SOPs, configures Augie to actual workflows
  • › Trains every dispatcher by name — tracks completion, not attendance
  • › Feeds field intel back to Product — edge cases, integration gaps, unmet needs
›
CS
  • › Runs structured QBRs — asks "can you say what changed in numbers?" every quarter
  • › Owns expansion scope — second workflow proposed at the 30-day ROI review
  • › Routes customer feedback to Product with commercial priority framing
›
PRODUCT
  • › Ingests FDE field intel and CS feedback into sprint prioritisation
  • › Ships features that unlock the next maturity stage for stalled accounts
  • › Publishes AE-ready release briefs so commercial teams can sell what just shipped
›
LEADERSHIP
  • › Reviews account health weekly — flags any account stuck at Stage 2 for 60+ days
  • › Holds CS accountable for NDR above 110% — not just renewal, expansion
  • › Prioritises product roadmap items that unlock the most stalled ARR
